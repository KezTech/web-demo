About Shree Shiv Academy

25 Years of Educational Excellence

Our Story

Founded in 2000, Shree Shiv Academy has been at the forefront of educational innovation for over two decades. What started as a small institution with a big vision has grown into one of the region's most respected educational establishments.

Our commitment to academic excellence, combined with a focus on character development and global citizenship, has made us the school of choice for families seeking comprehensive education.

We believe in nurturing not just academic prowess but also emotional intelligence, creativity, and leadership skills that prepare our students for success in an ever-changing world.

Our Mission

To provide a transformative educational experience that empowers students to become confident, compassionate, and capable leaders who will make positive contributions to society.

Our Vision

To be recognized globally as a center of educational excellence that nurtures innovative thinkers and responsible global citizens.

Our Journey

2000

Foundation

Shree Shiv Academy opens its doors with 50 students and a vision to transform education.

2005

First Expansion

New science laboratories and sports facilities added. Student body grows to 300.

2010

International Recognition

Received accreditation from International Education Board. Launched exchange programs.

2018

Digital Transformation

Introduced smart classrooms and 1:1 device program. Established innovation lab.

2025

Silver Jubilee

Celebrating 25 years with 1000+ students and planning for sustainable campus expansion.
